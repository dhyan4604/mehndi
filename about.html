About Shruti Pandya

Shruti Pandya is a professional mehndi artist known for elegant bridal, Arabic, and traditional Indian designs that blend fine detailing with modern styling.

Every design is customized to match the bride's outfit, event theme, and personal preferences so the final look feels unique, balanced, and memorable in photos and in person.

She uses fresh, natural henna ingredients and follows a clean, skin-safe process to ensure rich stain quality and comfortable application for all age groups.

Bridal Expertise

Detailed full-hand and full-leg bridal patterns with storytelling motifs.

Event Ready

Reliable mehndi services for engagements, festivals, and family functions.

Premium Finish

Neat line-work and balanced composition for dark, beautiful stain results.
